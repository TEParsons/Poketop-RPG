***Sleep Powder** Grass-type Status Move*: +2 to hit, puts opponent to sleep. 15PP.
#### Level 43

***Psychic** Psychic-type Special Move*: +5 to hit, d8+SpAtk damage. On a hit above 18 will lower opponent's Special Defence by 1. 10PP.
