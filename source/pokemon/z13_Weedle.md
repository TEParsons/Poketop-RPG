## Moves
#### Level 1

***Poison Sting** Poison-type Physical Move*: +5 to hit, d4+Atk damage. On a hit above 18 will poison the opponent.

***String Shot** Bug-type Status Move*: +4 to hit, lowers opponent's Speed by 2.
